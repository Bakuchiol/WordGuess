Word Duel

Wizards are just like us, they can end up fumbling a word or two, and with magic, that's pretty bad! So what's a spell caster to do?

Both duelists think of a word for the other to guess. Don't be misleading about it; even duels for fun needs honor!
Once you two have figured out what words you want to give your opponent, it's dueling time!
Whoever guesses their word's letters first wins!
So, are you ready for a word duel?!
